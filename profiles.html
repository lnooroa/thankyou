Profiles

Learn about the different types of people who may be interested in collaborative housing.

Young Professionals

Focused on urban living, close to work and social hubs. Typically interested in co-living or modern apartments.

Growing Families

Need space and comfort, often looking for suburban homes with gardens and community-friendly environments.

Retirees

Seeking downsizing opportunities, preferring low-maintenance living and close-knit communities.

Low-Income Renters

Focus on affordable housing, often through shared spaces or subsidized options.

High-Net-Worth Individuals

Interested in luxury accommodations with premium amenities and exclusive living environments.
